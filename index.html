Who We Are

ShareMeal is a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between food donors, NGOs, and communities in need.

We believe that no one should go hungry, especially during times of crisis. Our platform connects generous donors with verified NGOs, ensuring that food and resources reach those who need them most. During disasters, we mobilize our network to provide rapid relief and support to affected communities.

Through technology and community engagement, we're creating a more efficient and transparent food donation ecosystem.
