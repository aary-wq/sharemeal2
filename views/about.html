Our Mission

Connecting excess food with those in need, one meal at a time.

ShareMeal is dedicated to reducing food waste while fighting hunger in our communities. We believe that every person deserves access to nutritious meals, and no food should go to waste.

Our Story

Started in 2023, ShareMeal has grown from a simple idea to a movement.

What began as a small group of volunteers connecting local restaurants with homeless shelters has evolved into a comprehensive platform that brings together donors, NGOs, and those in need.
